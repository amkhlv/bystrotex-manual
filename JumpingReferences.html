Jumping to references

Remember the formula (1)? Clicking on (1) brings you to that formula.

It is also possible to jump to a particular slide, for example jump to “Installation”.

Sometimes you might want to press the “up” link, which will bring you to the title page. The title page has the list of contents, so you can jump to particular slides from there.
