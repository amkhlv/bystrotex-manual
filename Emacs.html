Racket mode

I use racket-mode.

I find it useful to disable smart bracket completion, because it does not play well with typesetting formulas. This requires some lines in my ‘Emacs‘ file:

(define-key racket-mode-map (kbd "]") nil)
(define-key racket-mode-map (kbd ")") nil)
(define-key racket-mode-map (kbd "}") nil)
